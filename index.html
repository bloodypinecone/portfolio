About me

Spring 2026 honors graduate from the University of Colorado Boulder with a B.A. in Computer Science, focusing on applied machine learning, and a minor in Philosophy with a focus on AI ethics.

I have been programming since I was 9 years old, and got my first job teaching computer science at 12 for Hands-On Technology Education, an organization that teaches coding to kids. Since then, I have:
- Worked on a team at Microsoft Research developing anomaly detection models to pinpoint seizures in epilepsy patients' MEG & EEG data
- Developed a YOLO object detection model to detect and classify pavement deformities for Arterial, a small tech startup in Boulder
- Wrote and taught machine learning lectures for CU Boulder Applied ML students
- Wrote computer science curriculum for San Diego Unified School District
- Designed quantum circuits at UC Irvine

I would love to pursue a career in ML model development & application, specifically computer vision or data analysis, and am open to any opportunities that would allow me to grow my skillset and contribute to meaningful projects.

Aside from computer science, I am also very interested in quantum mechanics and computation, and would love any opportunities to increase my knowledge in this field.
